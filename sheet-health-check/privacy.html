Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 24, 2026

This privacy policy describes how Sheet Health Check ("the Add-on") handles your data. We take your privacy seriously and designed this add-on to process everything locally with zero external data transfer.

What the Add-on Does

Sheet Health Check is a Google Sheets add-on that scans your spreadsheet for formula errors, inconsistencies, hardcoded values in formula columns, empty formula results, and performance issues.

Data Processing

All data processing happens entirely within Google's infrastructure. The add-on reads your spreadsheet data using only two API calls per sheet (getDisplayValues and getFormulas) to perform its analysis and displays results in a sidebar. No data is sent to any external server.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect or store personal information on external servers
  • We do not send any data to external services or APIs
  • We do not use cookies or tracking technologies
  • We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties
  • We do not use your data for advertising purposes
  • We do not collect analytics or usage tracking data

OAuth Scopes

The add-on requests only the spreadsheets.currentonly scope — the absolute minimum permission needed to read the current spreadsheet for analysis. This scope only allows access to the specific spreadsheet the add-on is installed in, not your entire Google Drive.

Data Retention

The add-on does not retain any data. Each scan runs fresh and results are displayed in the sidebar only during the current session. No scan results are stored anywhere.

Your Rights

  • You can uninstall the add-on at any time
  • You can revoke the add-on's access via your Google Account permissions

Children's Privacy

This Add-on is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact
